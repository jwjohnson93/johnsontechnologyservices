About Johnson Technology Services

Founded in August 2025, Johnson Technology Services was built on a straightforward idea: small and mid-sized businesses in Eastern North Carolina deserve the same quality of IT support as larger enterprises — without the enterprise price tag or the impersonal service.

Owner Jeremy Johnson holds degrees in Business Management and Information Technology, combining hands-on technical expertise with a clear understanding of what technology needs to actually accomplish for a business. JTS is local, responsive, and built for the long term.

  • Business-focused technology decisions, not just technical ones

  • Entra ID, identity management, and Microsoft 365 security expertise
